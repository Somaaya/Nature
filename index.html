The Nature is defined as everything found in the universe from various aspects, as it includes everything that God Almighty created, and man had no interference. The concept of nature is launched starting from the smallest element in the universe, which is the atom, to the outer world and the vast space, and includes the picturesque views of oceans, springs, forests, mountains, and all the creatures that live in the universe, in addition to the laws that govern and control the universe.

Elements of Nature:

The Water: is one of the most important basic elements that ensure the continuity of life, as nothing lives without water.

The Fire: is one of the most power ful energy elements, as it is a form of energy.

The Soil: is the name given to everything that takes the form of dirt, sand, stones, or crushed rocks. Soil forms the outer crust of planet Earth.

The Metal: Metals are in three states: hard metals such as iron, soft metals such as gold, and liquid metals such as mercury.

Other elements: such as animals, plants, and different terrains of mountains, valleys, and plains, in addition to air.
