Benefits of plants

  1. A source of food for humans and animals.
  2. The presence of plants on Earth leads to the renewal of oxygen on Earth.
  3. Plants contain essential nutrients for the human body, such as carbohydrates, vitamins, fiber, salts, proteins, and acids.
  4. Many medicines are extracted from plants to treat humans, and various perfumes.
  5. Plant cultivation provides a source of livelihood for the farmer, and it is also used in the fields of handicraft industry.
